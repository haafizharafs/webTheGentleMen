Troy McKubre (Solstate)

Solstate is the creation of Troy Mckubre. Troy has released 2 albums under the name Solstate.
'Whispers & Tremors' in 2008 and the self titled 'Solstate' in 2013 All recorded music is written, performed
and produced by Troy in collaboration with additional musicians and mix engineers.
Solstate have toured internationally as a live act and received commercial success.
